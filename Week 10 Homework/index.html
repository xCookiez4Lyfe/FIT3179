This is a map projection of koala data around Queensland in the year 2022

Map Description

This map shows the distribution of koalas in Queensland in the year 2022. Each dot represents the location of koalas different LGAs around Queensland The color of the circles represents the life status of the koalas, with grey indicating dead and green indicating alive.

Bar chart Desription

This bar chart visualises the frequency of dead and living koalas for each year of the dataset. It shows the breakdown between koalas that were found dead or alive to indicate how the koala population is changing by year.
