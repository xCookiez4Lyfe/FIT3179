Introduction

Queensland is home to the largest population of koalas in Australia. However, the koala population in Queensland has been declining due to a multitude of factors. This data visualisation explores the current state of koalas in Queensland, the threats they face, and the conservation efforts being made to protect them.

Koala sightings across Queensland between 2018 and 2022

The map above shows the number of koala sightings across Queensland between 2018 and 2022. The each circle represents the a koala sighting in a particular location. Sightings are recorded in each LGA and pinpointed using coordinates on the map.
